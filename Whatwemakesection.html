What is walnut face scrub, exactly? The name speaks for itself: Walnut face scrubs are formulated with crushed up walnuts (either the meat or shell). Enriched with stellar ingredients such as antioxidants and vitamin E, walnuts work over time to exfoliate dead skin cells, oil, dirt and other impurities stuck deep within your pores to prevent them from getting clogged.
